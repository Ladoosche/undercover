Undercover
A social deduction game
Civilians
Most players share a word. Describe it each round without being too obvious.
Undercovers
One or more players get a similar but different word. Blend in and avoid detection!
Mr. White (optional)
No word at all. Listen carefully and try to guess the civilian word if voted out.
How to play
Each round everyone gives a one-word clue, then votes to eliminate a suspect. Civilians win when all undercovers are gone. Undercovers win when no civilians remain.
