Undercover
A social deduction game
Civilians
Most players share a word. Describe it without being too obvious.
Undercovers
One or more players get a similar but different word. Blend in!
Mr. White
Optional role. No word at all — listen and try to guess.
How to play
Each round everyone gives a clue then vote to eliminate a suspect. Civilians win when undercovers are gone. Undercovers win when civilians are out.
